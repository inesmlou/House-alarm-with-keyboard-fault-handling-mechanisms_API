Implementation of an intrusion detection alarm system, in a restricted space as a single room retail store. The intrusion will be detected resorting to an infrared sensor, installed in such a way that it points towards the main entrance of the space to be protected. A switch is also installed on a window of the aforementioned space.
The Alarm has three main modes of operation, namely OFF, Presence Detector and Active. The three modes are selected by a three positions switch.

Part A - implementation of the general characteristics of the alarm 
Part B - implementation of an automated solution to the identification of a key pressed, on the available keyboard with 12 keys.
Part C - integration of the complete system, in GRAFCET
